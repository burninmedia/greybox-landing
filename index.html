What You Get

Real answers, not debug logs

Agent Audit Trail isn't another observability dashboard. It's the accountability infrastructure your agents are missing.

Decision Logs

Every agent decision captured with timestamp, inputs, outputs, and the full context window that informed it. No guesswork.

Context Snapshots

Exact state of everything the agent saw at decision time — memory, tool results, prior messages, external data. Full replay capability.

Chain-of-Thought Provenance

Trace the reasoning chain that led to each decision. Not just what the agent did, but what it considered and rejected along the way.

Compliance-Ready Exports

GDPR subject-access reports, EU AI Act Article 11 documentation, regulatory submissions — exportable in formats your compliance team actually needs.
